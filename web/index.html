How wallet sign-in works (read before connecting)

This site can only ever ask your wallet two things: eth_requestAccounts (see your address) and personal_sign (a free, gas-less signature). There is no transaction code in this site — it cannot move funds, request approvals, or spend gas. You'll be asked to sign exactly this message:

blobinhood.online wants you to sign in with your Ethereum account:
0xYourAddress

This signature only proves wallet ownership. It costs nothing, sends nothing, and grants this site no access to your funds.

URI: https://blobinhood.online
Version: 1
Chain ID: 4663
Nonce: (one-time code)
Issued At: (timestamp)

Your signature proves you own the wallet, so your cell spawns at the size of your bag and grows when your buys land on-chain. Or skip all of it and play anonymously — no wallet needed.
